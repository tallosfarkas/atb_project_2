\section{\texorpdfstring{\textbf{Task}}{Task}}\label{task}

\subsection{}\label{section}

Based on the rating history of 10,000 representative borrowers,
determine the one-year migration matrix based on the Lando and Skodeberg
procedure. In doing so, determine the migration matrix using the
generator matrix based on at least the first five terms.

Estimation of the generator matrix come from this formula:
\[\lambda_{ij} = \frac{N_{ij}}{\int Y_{i}(s)ds} \qquad i \neq j \qquad \text{and} \qquad \lambda_{ii} = -\sum_{j \neq i} \lambda_{ij}\]

where \(N_{ij}\) is the number of observed migrations from rating \(i\)
to rating \(j\) and \(Y_{i}(s)\) is the number of borrowers in rating
\(i\) at time \(s\). And the integral: \(\int Y_{i}(s)ds\) is the total
time spent by all borrowers in rating \(i\) during the observation
period. The one-year migration matrix can then be obtained by
exponentiating the generator matrix:
\(W(\Delta t) = e^{\Lambda \cdot \Delta t} = \sum_{k=0}^{\infty} \frac{(\Lambda \cdot \Delta t)^k}{k!}\)
